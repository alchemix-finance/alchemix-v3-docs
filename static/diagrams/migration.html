V3 Migration

Migration steps and Mana earning in one place.

Migration sequence · AIP-123
01
Freeze Protocol pause

V2 contracts are paused and all functions disabled, freezing every position in place.

02
Snapshot State capture

Every user's collateral and debt balance is recorded from the frozen contracts.

03
Asset Conversion Collateral move

Collateral is converted to USDC and wETH. The DAO treasury covers up to 0.25% slippage.

04
Positions V3 recreation

The Alchemix multisig recreates each position in V3, matching the exact collateral and debt from the snapshot. Positions under $0.01 are skipped.

05
Initialization V3 launch

Position NFTs are sent to each owner's wallet. V3 is live.
