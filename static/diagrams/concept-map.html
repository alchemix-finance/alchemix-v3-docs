Term definitions
alAsset
Synthetic debt token (alUSD, alETH). Minted when you borrow; 1 alAsset = 1 unit of debt inside the protocol.
Alchemist
Core smart contract. Accepts MYT collateral, issues alAsset loans, enforces LTV limits, and coordinates earmarking.
Mix-Yield Token (MYT)
ERC-20 collateral token representing a diversified yield strategy portfolio. Its value grows as strategies earn.
Transmuter
Accepts alAsset deposits and redeems them 1:1 for MYT after a fixed term. It's how the peg stays anchored.
LTV Ratio
Loan-to-value: debt ÷ collateral. Max borrow at 90%; liquidation triggered at 95%.
Fundamental Oracle
Values MYT by its underlying redemption value, not market price, so DEX swings can't trigger liquidations.
Earmarked Debt
Portion of a loan reserved during a redemption cycle. Collateral keeps earning until the moment of settlement.
Temporal Leverage
Extra yield earned because earmarked collateral keeps compounding between earmark (T₀) and settlement (T₁).
Redemption Rate
Annualised measure of debt paydown via Transmuter. = Annualised Transmuter volume ÷ Total system debt.
Self-Repaying Loan
A loan that repays itself. Vault yield and Transmuter redemptions chip away at the balance over time. No interest; the debt only ever goes down.
